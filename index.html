VULPES ♦ ♦ ♦

The forest welcomes you...

Vulpes is a free-to-play platformer projected for release in June 2023.

Working alongside the Norfolk Local Offer, the game and related materials are being produced with accessibility in mind.

This project between the Local Offer and CWA aims to provide more accessible and fun games for young people with SEND to enjoy.
